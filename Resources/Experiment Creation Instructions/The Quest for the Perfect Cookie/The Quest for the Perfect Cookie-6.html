Analyze data

How'd we do?

Take some bites of your new cookie. How did they turn out? Good? Tasty? Are they better than the first batch? Is it the perfect cookie?

Take a picture and compare it to the first batch.

Write down how they are different or similar, in terms of appearance, texture, and flavor. Is one lighter than the other? Tastier? Softer?

What are your conclusions?

What did you learn from this experiment? How did your specific variables change the cookie? Were they better or worse than the basic recipe?
