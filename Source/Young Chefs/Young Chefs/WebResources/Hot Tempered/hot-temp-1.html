Supplies

    Kitchen Equipment

  • 1 Stove
  • 1 Medium Sized Sauce Pan
  • 1 medium size bowl (preferably glass) that will fit atop medium size sauce pan
  • 1 spatula
  • 1 kitchen spoon
  • 1 cutting board
  • 1 knife
  • 2 paper plates
  • Celsius thermometer (preferably a candy thermometer; can be digital or analog, but must be very precise, and have a minimum temperature of 30 degrees C, and go up to 70 degrees C)

    Chocolate Fondue Ingredients

  • 250 Grams of Chocolate (70% cacao, in bars) (High quality such as Giardelli or Divinity brand recommended). 200 grams are for cooking, and 50 grams are for tasting.
  • 1 Banana
  • 5 Strawberries
  • 1 Pear or Mango
